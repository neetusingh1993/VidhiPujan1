Bhoomi Pujan

Bhoomi Puja is performed as a customary practice before constructing any building on construction site. It is done with the help of a priest who gives an auspicious date for the puja.

This Puja is mainly done at the north-east corner of the construction site. This Puja is performed for Goddess Bhoomi, Vastu Purush and Panch bhootas(five elements of nature).

This Pooja should be done by the head of the family with wife to receive blessings from mother earth to get rid of all the evils and negativities from the land. It also purifies the construction site.

This Pooja ensures smooth completion of construction work and well-being for the people who are going to live there. It also makes Vastu Purush happy.Click on the link below to read about bhoomi puja in detail…

Durga Pujan

Durga Puja, which starts from the sixth day of Navratri is celebrated as the victory over demon Mahisasur.Durga Puja/ Durgotsava is celebrated in the month of Ashvin as per Hindu Calendar and in the month of sept- oct as per Gregorian calendar. It is a ten days festival. Goddess Durga is worshiped in nine forms for nine days with each day dedicated to worship one form.3. Durga is the creative feminine force revered both for her gracious and terrifying form. Devotees perform this puja with great religious fervor and enthusiasm

On completing this Puja , Devi blesses the devotees higher compassion, wisdom, glory and power to deal with everyday hurdles of life. This Puja brings prosperity and wealth to the household and also empowers those devotees seeking spiritual advancements.3. This Puja creates a protective shield around devotees against evil eye, negative energies and brings good health and well being.
